ABOUT US

What We Make

We manufacture a broad range of tubular and coil products for energy and industrial applications:

​

  • Oil Country Tubular Goods (OCTG) – Welded casing, tubing, and premium connections

  • Line Pipe – ERW and HSAW pipe for oil, gas, hydrogen, and CO₂ transport

  • Hot Rolled Coil (HRC) – Used in heavy equipment, construction, and manufacturing

 

Annual capacity: 1.2 million tons of EAF-based steel

​

Facility footprint: Seven mills across Regina and Red Deer producing OCTG, line pipe, plate, and coil

01

Leading Edge Innovation

  • Supplied pipe for the Alberta Carbon Trunkline, one of the largest carbon capture pipelines in the world

  • Supporting pipelines that enable over 15 million tons of annual carbon capture capacity

  • Developing hydrogen-qualified pipe for 100% H₂ transmission

  • Innovating OCTG for zero-emission geothermal energy

  • Pioneering new materials and additives for high-pressure renewable energy systems

02

Powered by Sustainable Practices

  • Zero-liquid effluent facility in Regina: 100 percent of water is treated and recycled onsite, with zero wastewater discharge

  • Two EAFs reduce CO₂ emissions by up to 75 percent compared to traditional steelmaking

  • One of Western Canada’s largest scrap recyclers with fully-integrated production and recycling processes

03

Rooted in Community. Building the Region.

​

  • Employs approximately 1,800 people across Canadian operations

  • Represents around 2 percent of Saskatchewan’s GDP

  • Delivers economic value through sustainable industry and skill-based employment
